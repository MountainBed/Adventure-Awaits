Adventure Awaits!

Adventure Awaits! is an interactive choose-your-own-adventure story for students in grades 6 - 8. After choosing a story, students will go on an exciting adventure through time and space created collaboratively by their peers. Once they reach the end of the adventure and choose a direction to go in, they will continue the adventure in their own words.

Here's how it works:

  1. Choose your favorite genre and click on the button
  2. Read through the story
  3. When asked to make a decision, choose a direction and click the button
  4. Now for the fun part - continue writing the story from here!
